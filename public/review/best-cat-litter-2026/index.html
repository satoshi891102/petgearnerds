The Cat Litter Landscape in 2026

Cat litter is a $5 billion market in the US alone, and the options are overwhelming: clay, crystal, pine, corn, walnut, wheat, tofu, recycled paper. Each type claims to be the best. We cut through the marketing with actual testing.

The ideal cat litter does four things well: (1) clumps fast and hard for easy scooping, (2) controls odor for days without fragrance masking, (3) produces minimal dust, and (4) doesn't track across your house. No litter is perfect at all four, but the best ones get close.
