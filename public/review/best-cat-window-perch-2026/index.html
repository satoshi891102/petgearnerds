Window Perch Safety

  • Check suction cups weekly. A falling perch can injure your cat and destroy their confidence in the spot.
  • Don't exceed weight limits. If you have a 20-lb Maine Coon, get a perch rated for 50 lbs (safety margin).
  • Consider wall-mounted options for heavy cats or if you don't trust suction cups. Screw-in shelf brackets provide permanent, zero-maintenance support.
  • Window screens must be secure. An excited cat can push through a loose screen. Verify screen stability before installing a perch.
