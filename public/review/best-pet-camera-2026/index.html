Do You Need a Subscription?

Most pet cameras work fine without a subscription for live viewing and basic alerts. Subscriptions typically add: cloud video recording (so you can replay events), AI-powered alerts (person vs. pet vs. other motion), and bonus features like highlight reels.

Our recommendation: start without a subscription. If you find yourself wanting to replay events or getting too many false alerts, add the subscription. The eufy is the only camera that gives you full recording capability with no subscription (via local storage).
