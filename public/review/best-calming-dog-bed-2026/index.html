Beyond Beds: Managing Dog Anxiety

  • Exercise is #1: A tired dog is a calm dog. Most anxiety improves with adequate daily exercise (30-60 minutes minimum).
  • Routine matters: Dogs thrive on predictability. Consistent feeding, walking, and bedtime schedules reduce anxiety.
  • Adaptil diffusers: Synthetic "dog appeasing pheromone" has clinical evidence for reducing separation anxiety. Place near the bed.
  • Consult your vet: Severe anxiety may need behavioral training and/or medication. A calming bed helps but isn't a standalone treatment for clinical anxiety.
