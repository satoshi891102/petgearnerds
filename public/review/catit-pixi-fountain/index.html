Why Cat Fountains Matter

Cats are notoriously bad at staying hydrated. In the wild, cats get most of their water from prey. Domestic cats â€” especially those on dry food diets â€” often don't drink enough, leading to urinary tract issues, kidney problems, and chronic dehydration.
