Grooming Technique Tips

  • Always brush in the direction of fur growth â€” Against the grain causes discomfort and can break hair
  • Start with the least sensitive areas â€” Back and sides first, then chest, then belly (if they allow it)
  • Watch for signs of overstimulation â€” Tail twitching, ear flattening, skin rippling = stop
  • Make it a routine â€” Same time, same place. Cats thrive on predictability
  • Reward after every session â€” Treats, play, or whatever motivates your cat

How Often to Brush

  • Short-haired cats: Once per week is sufficient
  • Medium-haired cats: 2-3 times per week
  • Long-haired cats: Daily is ideal, minimum every other day
  • During shedding season (spring/fall): Increase frequency for all coat types
