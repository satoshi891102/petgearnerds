Why Enzymatic Cleaners Matter

Pet urine contains uric acid, which forms crystals that bind to surfaces. Standard cleaners wash away the liquid but leave the crystals behind. Those crystals reactivate in humidity, which is why cleaned spots sometimes smell again on rainy days. Enzymatic cleaners break down the uric acid molecules themselves â€” permanent removal, not masking.

The Golden Rule

Never use ammonia-based cleaners on pet stains. Urine contains ammonia â€” cleaning with ammonia actually attracts pets back to the same spot. Always use enzymatic or oxygen-based cleaners.
