Why Cats Ignore Some Scratchers

  • Too short: Cats need to fully extend their body while scratching. Under 26" is too short for most adult cats.
  • Unstable: If it wobbles or tips, cats won't use it. They need a solid surface to push against.
  • Wrong location: Place scratchers near where your cat already scratches (next to the couch they're destroying). Cats scratch to mark territory in high-traffic areas, not in back rooms.
  • Carpet material: Carpet-covered scratchers confuse cats â€” "I can scratch this carpet but not that carpet?" Sisal avoids this confusion.
  • Too clean: Don't replace a well-used scratcher too quickly. Cats return to their scent-marked scratching spots. The worn surface is a feature, not a bug.
