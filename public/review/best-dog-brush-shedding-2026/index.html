Shedding Management Beyond Brushing

  • Diet matters: Omega-3 fatty acids (fish oil) strengthen the coat and reduce excessive shedding. Look for dog foods with salmon or fish oil, or add a fish oil supplement.
  • Regular bathing: A bath every 4-6 weeks with deshedding shampoo loosens dead undercoat. Follow with thorough brushing while fur is damp.
  • Professional blowouts: During seasonal coat blows, a professional groomer's high-velocity dryer removes more loose fur in 30 minutes than you'll remove in a week of brushing at home.
  • See your vet if shedding is excessive: Sudden increase in shedding, bald patches, or skin irritation may indicate allergies, thyroid issues, or other medical conditions.
